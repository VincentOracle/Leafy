About Us

At Leafy, we are more than just an online marketplace for fresh vegetables; we are advocates for healthier lifestyles and champions of local farmers. Founded with a passion for wholesome eating and sustainable living, Leafy is committed to connecting communities with the bounty of Kenya's rich agricultural heritage. Our mission is simple: to provide access to the freshest, highest-quality produce while supporting local growers and promoting environmental stewardship. With a dedication to transparency, integrity, and customer satisfaction, Leafy strives to be your trusted partner in nourishing both body and soul. Join us in cultivating a greener, healthier future—one leaf at a time.
